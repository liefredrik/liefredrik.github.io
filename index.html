Welcome to the RedZen and Bluezen support page. Here you can find answers to common questions and get in touch with our support team.

FAQs

Q: How do I start the RedZen meditation timer?
A: Simply open the RedZen app, set your desired time, and press "Start".

Q: Why is the text in red?
A: Red text helps preserve night vision. It's easier on the eyes in dark settings, making it perfect for nighttime meditation.
